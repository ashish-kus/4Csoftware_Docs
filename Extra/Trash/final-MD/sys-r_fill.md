## sys.r_fill()
### Purpose
sys.r_fill() will right pad `<originalstring>` with `<fillchar>` up to length `<maxlen>`

### Usage
`<aret> = sys.r_fill(<originalstring>,<fillchar>,<maxlen>);`

### Arguments
- alpha `<originalstring>` - The alpha variable to right fill.
- alpha `<fillchar>` - An alpha variable of length 1 to use as the fill char.
- integer `<maxlen>` - The maximum length to fill `<originalstring>` to.

### Returns
- alpha `<aret>` - A copy of `<originalstring>` right filled with `<fillchar>` to a length of `<maxlen>`

### Where Used
sys.r_fill() can be called from anywhere.
