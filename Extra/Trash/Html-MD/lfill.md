sys.l_fill()

### sys.l_fill()

Purpose:

sys.l_fill() will left pad \<originalstring\> with \<fillchar\> up to
length \<maxlen\>

Usage:

\<aret\> = sys.l_fill(\<originalstring\>,\<fillchar\>,\<maxlen\>);

Arguments:

alpha \<originalstring\> - The alpha variable to left fill.

Arguments: alpha \<fillchar\> - An alpha variable of length 1 to use as
the fill char.

Arguments: integer \<maxlen\> - The maximum length to fill
\<originalstring\> to.

Returns:

alpha \<aret\> - A copy of \<originalstring\> left filled with
\<fillchar\> to a length of \<maxlen\>

Where Used:

sys.l_fill() can be called from anywhere.
